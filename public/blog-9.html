Common Crypto Scams to Watch For

Fake Reward Platforms

Sites that promise huge returns but require deposits or personal information.

Phishing Attacks

Fake emails or websites pretending to be legitimate crypto services.

Ponzi Schemes

Platforms that focus more on recruitment than actual product value.

How to Identify Legitimate Platforms

  • No requirement for private keys or seed phrases
  • Transparent business model
  • Realistic earning expectations
  • Positive user reviews and community
  • Clear terms of service and privacy policy
